# Subcontractor Onboarding Checklist

**Checklist Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Purpose:** Ensure all Subcontractor onboarding steps completed before first job assignment.

---

## Pre-Onboarding

- [ ] **Subcontractor identified:** Name, contact information, experience verified
- [ ] **Background check:** Criminal record check completed (if required)
- [ ] **References:** References checked (if required)

---

## Contract and Legal

- [ ] **Independent Subcontractor Agreement (Longform):** Signed by both parties
- [ ] **Rate Sheet Template:** Signed and attached to agreement
- [ ] **Sub Scope Will/Won't Do:** Reviewed and acknowledged
- [ ] **Sub Onboarding Checklist:** Completed and signed

---

## Insurance and Compliance

- [ ] **General liability insurance:** COI provided to Company ($[X] million minimum)
- [ ] **WSIB coverage:** WSIB clearance certificate provided (if applicable)
- [ ] **HST number:** HST number provided (if applicable)
- [ ] **Insurance expiry:** Expiry date noted; renewal reminder set

---

## Portal Access

- [ ] **Portal account created:** Email, password set
- [ ] **Role assigned:** VENDOR_WORKER or INTERNAL_WORKER
- [ ] **WorkforceAccount assigned:** Subcontractor assigned to WorkforceAccount
- [ ] **Portal training:** Subcontractor trained on portal use (job list, completion submission)

---

## Training and Documentation

- [ ] **SOPs reviewed:** SOP_01 through SOP_06 reviewed and acknowledged
- [ ] **Policies reviewed:** POL_01 through POL_06 reviewed and acknowledged
- [ ] **Checklist templates:** CL_01 through CL_08 reviewed
- [ ] **Photo requirements:** Schedule D (QA and Evidence Requirements) reviewed
- [ ] **Site access:** Schedule C (Access and Site Rules) reviewed

---

## Site-Specific Onboarding

**For each site assigned:**

- [ ] **Site scope:** Schedule A (Site Scope Template) reviewed
- [ ] **Access credentials:** Keys, FOBs, or codes provided and tested
- [ ] **Site rules:** Schedule C (Access and Site Rules) reviewed
- [ ] **Checklist template:** Site-specific checklist template assigned
- [ ] **Supplies:** Supply responsibility confirmed (COMPANY / CLIENT / MIXED)
- [ ] **Service window:** Service window confirmed
- [ ] **Parking:** Parking location confirmed

---

## Equipment and Supplies

- [ ] **Supplies provided:** If COMPANY or MIXED, supplies provided and location confirmed
- [ ] **Equipment provided:** If COMPANY or MIXED, equipment provided and tested
- [ ] **Mobile device:** Subcontractor has mobile device for portal access and photo upload

---

## First Job Assignment

- [ ] **First job assigned:** Test job assigned (if applicable)
- [ ] **Completion submitted:** First completion submitted successfully
- [ ] **Approval process:** Approval process explained and tested
- [ ] **Payment process:** Payment process explained

---

## Sign-Off

**Subcontractor acknowledges:**

- [ ] I have reviewed all SOPs and policies
- [ ] I understand my responsibilities and scope
- [ ] I understand payment terms and approval process
- [ ] I understand non-solicitation and confidentiality obligations
- [ ] I am ready to begin work
