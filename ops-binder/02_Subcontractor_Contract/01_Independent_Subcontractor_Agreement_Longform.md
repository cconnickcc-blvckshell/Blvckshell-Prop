## 4. Scope of Services

**Services provided:**

- Common area cleaning per Company client sites
- Per Company SOPs and policies (provided to Subcontractor)
- Per site-specific checklists and requirements
- Add-on services (if approved per Schedule B)
- Light maintenance (if approved per Schedule B)

**Subcontractor will:**

- Perform services per Company standards
- Follow Company SOPs and policies
- Complete checklists per site requirements
- Provide photo evidence per Schedule D
- Maintain professional conduct per POL_04

**Subcontractor will not:**

- Contact clients directly (all communication via Company)
- Solicit clients or residents
- Perform licensed trade work
- Perform work outside scope without approval

## 5. Standards and Quality

**Quality standards:**

- Per POL_01 (Quality Standard and Rework)
- All required checklist items must be completed
- Minimum photo count must be met
- Visual standards must be met (no debris, streaks, smudges)

**Photo proof requirement:**

- Per Schedule D (QA and Evidence Requirements)
- Minimum photo count per job (default: 4)
- Mandatory photo items per checklist
- Photos must show completion
- Photos retained per DATA_RETENTION policy (90 days default)

**Rework:**

- If completion rejected, Subcontractor must correct deficiencies
- No additional payment for rework
- Repeated failures may result in removal from site or termination

## 6. Payment Terms

**Payment per job:**

- Payment only for approved completions
- Job must be approved by Company admin before payment
- Payment amount per Rate Sheet Template (Schedule B)

**Payment timing:**

- Payment per payout batch (bi-weekly or monthly, per Company schedule)
- Payment after job approved and included in payout batch
- No payment for rejected completions until corrected and approved

**Right to withhold:**

- Company may withhold payment for deficiencies
- Company may withhold payment if Subcontractor breaches agreement
- Withheld amounts paid after deficiencies corrected or breach resolved

**HST:**

- Subcontractor responsible for HST (if applicable)
- Subcontractor must provide HST number if applicable
- Company pays Subcontractor amount plus HST (if Subcontractor is HST-registered)

## 9. Non-Solicitation and Non-Circumvention

### Non-Solicitation Period

**Duration:** 24 months after termination of this agreement

**Prohibited during non-solicitation period:**

1. **Client solicitation:**
   - No soliciting clients introduced by Company
   - No contacting clients directly
   - No offering competing services to Company clients

2. **Site solicitation:**
   - No soliciting sites where Subcontractor performed services
   - No contacting property management directly
   - No offering competing services to Company sites

3. **Worker solicitation:**
   - No soliciting Company workers to leave
   - No recruiting Company workers for competing business

---

### Non-Circumvention

**Prohibited:**

- **Circumventing Company:** No going around Company to contact clients directly
- **Direct relationships:** No establishing direct relationships with clients
- **Competing services:** No offering competing services to Company clients

**Exception:** Subcontractor may work for clients not introduced by Company (must prove no Company introduction).

## 10. Remedies for Breach

### Injunctive Relief

**Company may seek:**

- **Injunction:** Court order preventing solicitation/circumvention
- **Immediate enforcement:** No waiting period
- **Geographic scope:** Windsor–Essex and surrounding areas

---

### Liquidated Damages

**Per breach:**

- **Per client/site:** $[X] per client/site solicited
- **Genuine pre-estimate:** Amount reflects genuine loss estimate
- **Not penalty:** Enforceable as liquidated damages (not penalty)

**Calculation:**
- Based on lost revenue per client/site
- Reasonable and enforceable
- Per client/site solicited or circumvented

**Example:** If Subcontractor solicits Client A, liquidated damages = $[X] per client/site solicited.

---

### Termination

**Immediate termination for:**

1. **Solicitation breach:**
   - Soliciting clients or residents
   - Establishing direct relationships
   - Violating non-solicitation period

2. **Repeated quality failures:**
   - 3+ consecutive rejections on same site
   - Pattern of incomplete or substandard work

3. **Conduct violations:**
   - Solicitation of residents
   - Unprofessional behavior
   - Violation of site rules

4. **Gross misconduct:**
   - Theft or property damage
   - Safety violations
   - Legal violations

## 11. Termination

- [30-60] days written notice
- Notice via email or registered mail
- Services continue during notice period

- Material breach of contract
- Repeated quality failures
- Conduct violations
