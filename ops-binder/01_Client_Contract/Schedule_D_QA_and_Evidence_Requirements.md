# Schedule D: QA and Evidence Requirements

**Schedule Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Purpose:** Define quality assurance requirements, photo evidence standards, and inspection cadence. Attached to Master Service Agreement.

---

## Photo Evidence Requirements

### Minimum Photo Count

**Per job:** [X] photos minimum (default: 4)  
**Per area:** Per checklist templates (CL_01 through CL_08)  
**Photo cap:** 20 photos per job maximum  
**Photo retention:** 90 days default (per DATA_RETENTION policy)

---

### Mandatory Photo Items

**Per checklist template:**

- **Lobby (CL_01):** Floor, glass doors, desk, bins (minimum 4)
- **Hallway (CL_02):** Floor, baseboards, bins, overall view (minimum 4)
- **Stairwell (CL_03):** Steps, handrails, baseboards, overall view (minimum 4)
- **Elevator (CL_04):** Floor, control panel, walls, mirror (minimum 4)
- **Garbage Room (CL_05):** Floor (mandatory), bins (mandatory), walls, overall view (minimum 4)
- **Glass Doors (CL_06):** Glass (mandatory), frames, threshold (minimum 3)
- **Washroom (CL_07):** Floor (mandatory), toilets (mandatory), sinks, mirrors (minimum 4)
- **Seasonal Deep Clean (CL_08):** Per checklist requirements

---

### Photo Quality Standards

**Photo requirements:**

- Clear, well-lit
- Show completion (e.g., clean floor, empty bins)
- Timestamped automatically by device
- No resident faces or unit numbers visible (if avoidable)
- Per POL_03 (Privacy Photo Handling and Data)

## Completion Checklists

### Checklist Requirements

**Per job completion:**

- All required checklist items answered (PASS / FAIL / NA)
- No TBD or incomplete items
- Notes added where needed (FAIL items, maintenance issues)
- Checklist template per site (CL_01 through CL_08)

**Checklist submission:**
- Submitted via portal
- Status: COMPLETED_PENDING_APPROVAL
- Admin reviews and approves/rejects

---

## Quality Assurance Inspection

### Inspection Cadence

**Inspection frequency:** [Monthly, Quarterly, Per complaint]  
**Inspection method:** [Admin inspection, Client walk-through, Photo review]

**If monthly:**
- Admin inspects site monthly
- Reviews completions, photos, notes
- Documents findings per QA_01 (Site Inspection Form)

**If quarterly:**
- Admin inspects site quarterly
- Reviews completions, photos, notes
- Documents findings per QA_01 (Site Inspection Form)

**If per complaint:**
- Admin inspects when Client complaint received
- Reviews completions, photos, notes
- Documents findings per QA_01 (Site Inspection Form)

---

### Inspection Process

**Step 1:** Admin schedules inspection  
**Step 2:** Admin inspects site:
- Reviews areas cleaned
- Checks quality standards
- Reviews photo evidence
- Documents findings

**Step 3:** Admin completes QA_01 (Site Inspection Form):
- Areas inspected
- Quality findings
- Deficiencies identified (if any)
- Recommendations

**Step 4:** Admin shares findings:
- With Client (if requested)
- With workers (if deficiencies found)
- Documented in audit log

---

## Quality Standards

### Pass Criteria

**Job passes if:**

- All required checklist items marked PASS
- No visible debris, streaks, smudges, or residue
- Bins emptied and clean (if applicable)
- No unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met
- Mandatory photo items captured

**Per POL_01 (Quality Standard and Rework)**

---

### Fail Criteria

**Job fails if:**

- Checklist items marked FAIL (without documented reason)
- Visible debris, streaks, smudges, or residue
- Bins not emptied or visibly dirty
- Unpleasant odors present
- Minimum photo count not met
- Mandatory photo items missing

**Rework required:** Per POL_01 (Quality Standard and Rework)
