## Scope of Work

### Areas Cleaned

**Common areas only (not inside resident units):**

1. **Lobby** (CL_01)
   - Floor cleaning (sweep, mop)
   - Glass doors (spot clean)
   - Desk/counter surfaces
   - Waste bins (empty and clean)
   - High-touch surfaces (sanitize)

2. **Hallways** (CL_02)
   - Floor cleaning (sweep, mop)
   - Baseboards (spot clean)
   - Waste bins (empty and clean)
   - High-touch surfaces (sanitize)

3. **Stairwells** (CL_03)
   - Steps (sweep, mop)
   - Handrails (clean and sanitize)
   - Baseboards (spot clean)
   - High-touch surfaces (sanitize)

4. **Elevators** (CL_04)
   - Floor cleaning (sweep, mop)
   - Control panel (sanitize)
   - Walls (spot clean)
   - Mirrors (if applicable)

5. **Garbage Rooms** (CL_05)
   - Floor cleaning (sweep, mop)
   - Bins (empty and clean)
   - Walls (spot clean)
   - Deodorize

6. **Glass Doors** (CL_06)
   - Glass (spot clean)
   - Frames (spot clean)
   - Thresholds (sweep)

7. **Washrooms** (CL_07)
   - Floor cleaning (sweep, mop)
   - Toilets (clean and sanitize)
   - Sinks (clean and sanitize)
   - Mirrors (clean)
   - High-touch surfaces (sanitize)

8. **Seasonal Deep Clean** (CL_08)
   - Per frequency (monthly, quarterly, etc.)
   - Deep clean of all areas
   - Per checklist CL_08

---

### Areas NOT Cleaned

**Out of scope:**

- Resident units (inside units)
- Unit doors (common-area side only)
- Licensed trade work (electrical, plumbing, HVAC, fire, elevator)
- Structural repairs
- Pest control
- Landscaping
- Snow removal
