## Pricing Notes

**Pricing structure:**
- Fixed pricing per add-on (unless hourly noted)
- Emergency surcharge may apply (bio cleanup, after-hours)
- Minimum call-out applies to light maintenance

**Approval required:**
- All add-ons require approval before work performed
- Admin creates work order (status: REQUESTED)
- Admin approves (status: APPROVED)
- Worker executes per SOPs
- Admin invoices after completion
