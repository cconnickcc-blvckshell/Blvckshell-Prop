# POL_05: Communications and Client Contact

**Policy Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Purpose:** Define communication boundaries: who communicates with clients, how, and when. Ensures consistent messaging and prevents unauthorized client contact.

---

## Communication Boundaries

### Who Communicates with Clients

**ONLY ADMIN communicates with clients:**

- **Property management:** Admin only
- **Site contacts:** Admin only
- **Client representatives:** Admin only

**Workers DO NOT communicate with clients:**
- No direct contact with property management
- No direct contact with site contacts
- No direct contact with client representatives
- All communication via admin

---

### Worker Communication

**Workers communicate ONLY with admin:**

- **Job-related:** Via portal (job notes, completion submission)
- **Issues:** Via portal (incident reports, messages)
- **Urgent:** Via phone (per admin instructions)
- **No client contact:** Never contact clients directly

---

## Communication Channels

### Admin → Client

**Channels:**

1. **Email:** Primary channel for non-urgent communication
2. **Phone:** For urgent issues or scheduled calls
3. **Portal (future):** Client portal (Phase 2+, not Phase 1)
4. **In-person:** Site walks, meetings (admin only)

**Content:**
- Job scheduling and changes
- Quality issues and resolutions
- Incident reports and resolutions
- Billing and invoicing
- Contract updates

---

### Worker → Admin

**Channels:**

1. **Portal:** Primary channel (job notes, incident reports, messages)
2. **Phone:** Urgent issues only (per admin instructions)

**Content:**
- Job completion submissions
- Incident reports
- Access issues
- Quality concerns
- Maintenance issues

---

### Worker → Resident (Limited)

**Permitted interactions:**

- **Greeting:** Polite greeting if resident approaches
- **Explanation:** "I'm cleaning the common areas today."
- **Direction:** Direct maintenance questions to property management

**Prohibited:**
- No discussing company business
- No discussing pricing
- No accepting requests for additional work
- No providing contact information

---

## Communication Procedures

### Job-Related Communication

**Worker → Admin:**

**Step 1:** Complete job per checklist  
**Step 2:** Submit completion via portal:
- Checklist answers
- Photo evidence
- Notes (if needed)

**Step 3:** Admin reviews and responds:
- Approves or rejects completion
- Provides feedback if needed

**Step 4:** If rejected:
- Worker receives rejection reason
- Worker corrects and resubmits
- Admin approves when corrected

---

### Incident Communication

**Worker → Admin:**

**Step 1:** Identify incident (safety, property damage, biohazard, etc.)  
**Step 2:** Document via incident report:
- Type, description, location, photos
- Submit immediately

**Step 3:** Contact admin if urgent:
- Safety hazards: Call immediately
- Property damage: Call within 1 hour
- Other: Portal message within 4 hours

**Step 4:** Admin coordinates:
- Contacts client if needed
- Coordinates resolution
- Updates worker when safe to proceed

---

### Client Complaint Communication

**Client → Admin:**

**Step 1:** Client contacts admin (property management)  
**Step 2:** Admin receives complaint:
- Documents complaint
- Investigates (reviews completion, photos, notes)

**Step 3:** Admin responds to client:
- Acknowledges complaint
- Explains resolution (reclean, credit, etc.)
- Provides timeline

**Step 4:** Admin coordinates resolution:
- Assigns reclean if needed
- Issues credit if appropriate
- Updates worker if needed

**Worker involvement:**
- Worker does not communicate with client
- Admin communicates resolution to worker
- Worker executes reclean if assigned

---

## Response Time Expectations

### Admin Response Times

**Urgent (safety hazards):**
- Response: Immediate (within 15 minutes)
- Resolution: As soon as possible (same day if possible)

**Standard (non-urgent):**
- Response: Within 4 business hours
- Resolution: Within 1-2 business days

**Client complaints:**
- Response: Within 1 business day
- Resolution: Within 2-3 business days

---

### Worker Response Times

**Job completion:**
- Submit within service window (same day as job)
- Resubmit if rejected: Within 24 hours

**Incident reports:**
- Submit immediately (within service window)
- Contact admin if urgent: Immediately

---

## Escalation Procedures

### When to Escalate

**Worker escalates to admin if:**

1. **Access denied:** Cannot enter site
2. **Safety hazard:** Unsafe condition discovered
3. **Incident:** Any incident requiring admin attention
4. **Unclear instructions:** Not sure how to proceed
5. **Client contact attempt:** Client tries to contact worker directly

**How to escalate:**
- Via portal message (preferred)
- Via phone if urgent (per admin instructions)
- Document all escalations

---

### Admin Escalation

**Admin escalates to client if:**

1. **Site access issues:** Credential problems, site changes
2. **Safety hazards:** Requiring site attention
3. **Property damage:** Requiring client coordination
4. **Contract issues:** Scope, pricing, terms

**How to escalate:**
- Email (preferred)
- Phone if urgent
- In-person meeting if needed

---

## Prohibited Communications

### Workers Must Not

**Prohibited:**

1. **Direct client contact:**
   - No emailing clients
   - No calling clients
   - No texting clients
   - No meeting clients (except admin-introduced)

2. **Social media:**
   - No posting about clients or sites
   - No identifying clients on social media
   - No discussing company business on social media

3. **Third-party communication:**
   - No discussing company business with competitors
   - No sharing client information
   - No sharing company procedures

## Communication Templates

### Worker → Admin: Access Denied

**Template:**
"Access denied at [site name]. Used credentials [key/FOB/code]. Tried [X] times. No response from site contact. Please coordinate access or reschedule."

---

### Worker → Admin: Incident Report

**Template:**
"Incident at [site name], [location]. Type: [SAFETY/PROPERTY_DAMAGE/BIOHAZARD/LOST_KEY/OTHER]. Description: [details]. Photos attached. Stopped work per SOP. Awaiting instructions."

---

### Admin → Client: Quality Issue Resolution

**Template:**
"Thank you for reporting the quality issue at [site name]. We've reviewed the completion and authorized a reclean at no charge. Reclean scheduled for [date]. We apologize for the inconvenience."
