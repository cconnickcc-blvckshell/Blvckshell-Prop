# SOP_02: Site Access and Conduct

**SOP Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026  
**Purpose:** Procedures for site access, credential management, and worker conduct. Ensures security and professionalism.

---

## Pre-Visit: Access Credential Verification

**Before leaving for site:**

- [ ] Verify access credentials assigned:
  - Key (physical key)
  - FOB (keycard/fob)
  - Code (access code)
- [ ] Check credential status in portal (ACTIVE / LOST / RETURNED)
- [ ] If credential missing or lost:
  - **DO NOT** proceed to site
  - Contact admin immediately
  - Document lost credential via incident report (type: LOST_KEY)

---

## Arrival and Entry

### Standard Entry Procedure

**Step 1.1:** Arrive at scheduled time (within service window)  
**Step 1.2:** Park in designated area (per site instructions)  
**Step 1.3:** Use access credentials per site Schedule C:
- **Key:** Insert and turn; return key to secure location after entry
- **FOB:** Tap at reader; wait for beep/green light
- **Code:** Enter code at keypad; wait for unlock

**Step 1.4:** Enter site and verify correct location

---

### Entry Denied: Decision Tree

**If access denied:**

1. **Verify credentials:**
   - Check key/FOB/code is correct
   - Try again (may be temporary glitch)

2. **If still denied:**
   - **DO NOT** force entry
   - **DO NOT** wait indefinitely
   - Contact admin immediately
   - Document via incident report (type: OTHER; description: "Access denied with valid credentials")

3. **Admin will:**
   - Verify credential status
   - Coordinate with site contact if needed
   - Provide alternative access or reschedule

4. **Worker action:**
   - Wait for admin instruction
   - Do not proceed until access confirmed

---

## Credential Management

### During Visit

**Step 2.1:** Keep credentials secure:
- Do not leave keys/FOBs unattended
- Do not share credentials with unauthorized persons
- Do not photograph or share access codes

**Step 2.2:** If credential lost or stolen during visit:
- **STOP WORK**
- Contact admin immediately
- Document via incident report (type: LOST_KEY)
- Admin will deactivate credential and issue replacement

---

### Post-Visit: Credential Return

**If credentials must be returned (per site policy):**

**Step 3.1:** Return credential to designated location:
- Key drop box
- Concierge desk
- Admin pickup

**Step 3.2:** Verify return logged in portal (credential status: RETURNED)

**Step 3.3:** If return location unclear:
- Contact admin
- Do not leave credential unattended

---

## Resident Interaction Rules

### Permitted Interactions

**You MAY:**
- Greet residents politely if they approach
- Explain you're cleaning common areas
- Answer questions about cleaning schedule (general only)
- Direct maintenance questions to property management

**Example:** "Good morning, I'm cleaning the common areas today. Is there anything I should be aware of?"

---

### Prohibited Interactions

**You MUST NOT:**
- **Solicit residents** for personal services or business
- **Discuss pricing** or company rates
- **Accept gifts** or tips from residents
- **Enter resident units** (even if invited)
- **Share personal contact information**
- **Post photos** of site or residents on social media
- **Complain** about site conditions or management to residents

**Violation consequences:**
- Immediate removal from site
- Potential termination
- Legal action if solicitation breach (per subcontractor agreement)

---

### If Resident Approaches with Complaint

**Step 4.1:** Listen politely and professionally  
**Step 4.2:** Do not make promises or commitments  
**Step 4.3:** Direct to property management:
- "I'll note that for our records. Please also contact property management directly."

**Step 4.4:** Document in job notes:
- Resident concern
- Area affected
- Time/date

**Step 4.5:** Admin will follow up with property management

---

## Escalations

### When to Escalate

**Escalate to admin immediately if:**

1. **Access denied** with valid credentials
2. **Safety hazard** discovered (see SOP_04)
3. **Credential lost or stolen**
4. **Resident complaint** about cleaning quality (document and escalate)
5. **Property damage** discovered (see SOP_04)
6. **Biohazard** encountered (see SOP_04)
7. **Resident solicitation attempt** (report immediately)
8. **Any situation** where you're unsure how to proceed

**How to escalate:**
- Contact admin via portal message or phone (per admin instructions)
- Document via incident report if applicable
- Do not proceed until admin responds

---

## Prohibited Behaviors

**The following behaviors are strictly prohibited:**

- **Solicitation:** No soliciting residents for personal services or business
- **Pricing discussions:** No discussing company rates or pricing
- **Gifts:** No accepting gifts, tips, or favors from residents
- **Social media:** No posting photos or comments about sites or residents
- **Unit entry:** No entering resident units (even if invited)
- **Unauthorized access:** No accessing areas not in scope
- **Substance use:** No alcohol or drugs on site
- **Smoking:** No smoking on site (unless designated area per site rules)
- **Personal calls:** Minimize personal phone calls during work
- **Loitering:** Complete work and leave site promptly

**Violation consequences:**
- First offense: Warning and retraining
- Second offense: Removal from site
- Third offense: Termination
- Solicitation breach: Immediate termination + legal action per subcontractor agreement

---

## Site-Specific Rules

**Each site may have additional rules (per Schedule C):**

- **Parking:** Designated areas only
- **Service windows:** Work only during approved hours
- **Noise:** Minimize noise during quiet hours
- **Concierge:** Check in/out procedures
- **Elevator use:** Service mode or normal operation
- **Supplies:** Where to store/access supplies

**Worker responsibility:**
- Review site-specific rules before each visit
- Follow all site rules
- Ask admin if rules unclear

---

## Post-Visit: Secure Site

**Step 5.1:** Verify all areas cleaned per checklist  
**Step 5.2:** Remove "Cleaning in Progress" signage  
**Step 5.3:** Secure site:
- Lock all doors
- Turn off lights if required
- Return keys to secure location (if applicable)

**Step 5.4:** Leave site promptly after completion

---

## Decision Tree: Common Scenarios

### Scenario: Resident Asks You to Clean Their Unit
→ **Politely decline:** "I only clean common areas. Please contact property management for unit cleaning services."

### Scenario: Resident Offers You a Tip
→ **Politely decline:** "Thank you, but I cannot accept tips. I appreciate the gesture."

### Scenario: Resident Complains About Previous Cleaning
→ **Listen professionally** → **Document in notes** → **Escalate to admin** → **Do not make promises**

### Scenario: You See Another Worker Soliciting Residents
→ **Report immediately to admin** → **Document via incident report**

### Scenario: Access Code Doesn't Work
→ **Try once more** → **If still fails, contact admin** → **Do not proceed until access confirmed**
