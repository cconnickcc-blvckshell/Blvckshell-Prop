# Garbage Room Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_05  
**Area:** Garbage Room / Waste Disposal Area  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **GRB-001** | Sweep and mop floor (no visible debris, residue, or spills) | Yes | Floor after cleaning | Visible debris, residue, or spills |
| **GRB-002** | Clean all waste/recycling bins (inside and out; sanitized) | Yes | Bins after cleaning | Bins visibly dirty or not sanitized |
| **GRB-003** | Wipe down bin lids and handles (sanitized, no sticky residue) | Yes | Lids/handles | Sticky residue or visible dirt |
| **GRB-004** | Clean walls (wipe down, no smudges, splatters, or residue) | Yes | Walls | Smudges, splatters, or residue |
| **GRB-005** | Clean door handles and light switches (sanitized, no smudges) | Yes | Handles/switches | Smudges or visible dirt |
| **GRB-006** | Remove and dispose of any overflow garbage (if present) | Yes | Overflow area | Overflow garbage present |
| **GRB-007** | Check for and remove cobwebs (corners, ceiling) | Yes | Corners/ceiling | Cobwebs visible |
| **GRB-008** | Ensure garbage room is free of odors (use deodorizer if needed) | Yes | N/A | Unpleasant odors present |
| **GRB-009** | Report any pest activity or maintenance issues | No | N/A | N/A (note if issues found) |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** GRB-001, GRB-002, GRB-003, GRB-005, GRB-006, GRB-008
- **2x/week:** GRB-004, GRB-007
- **As needed:** GRB-009 (pest/maintenance reporting)

---

## Photo Requirements

**Minimum photos per visit:**
- 1 photo: Floor after cleaning (GRB-001) — **REQUIRED**
- 1 photo: Bins after cleaning (GRB-002) — **REQUIRED**
- 1 photo: Walls (GRB-004)
- 1 photo: Overall garbage room view

**Total minimum:** 4 photos per garbage room visit (floor and bins are mandatory)

---

## Definition of Done

Garbage room is considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- Floor free of debris, residue, and spills
- All bins cleaned and sanitized (inside and out)
- No overflow garbage
- No unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met (especially floor and bins)

---

## Notes

- **High priority:** Garbage rooms are high-traffic, high-odor areas; thorough cleaning is critical
- Use appropriate cleaning chemicals and deodorizers
- Report pest activity immediately via incident report
- Report overflow or maintenance issues (broken bins, leaks) to admin
- Wear appropriate PPE (gloves, mask) when cleaning garbage rooms
