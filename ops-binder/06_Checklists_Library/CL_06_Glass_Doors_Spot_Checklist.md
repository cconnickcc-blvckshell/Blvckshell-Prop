# Glass Doors Spot Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_06  
**Area:** Glass Doors / Windows (Spot Clean)  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **GLD-001** | Clean glass doors/windows (streak-free, no fingerprints, smudges, or water spots) | Yes | Glass after cleaning | Streaks, fingerprints, smudges, or water spots visible |
| **GLD-002** | Clean door frames and handles (wipe down, no smudges or residue) | Yes | Frames/handles | Smudges or residue visible |
| **GLD-003** | Clean threshold/entry mat (sweep or vacuum, no debris) | Yes | Threshold/mat | Debris visible |
| **GLD-004** | Wipe down door hardware (locks, hinges if accessible) | Yes | Hardware | Visible dirt or smudges |
| **GLD-005** | Ensure glass is free of streaks when viewed from both sides | Yes | Glass (both sides) | Streaks visible from either side |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** GLD-001, GLD-002, GLD-003
- **2x/week:** GLD-004
- **As needed:** GLD-005 (spot check for streaks)

---

## Definition of Done

Glass doors are considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- Glass streak-free and free of fingerprints, smudges, and water spots
- Door frames and handles clean
- Threshold/mat free of debris
- Minimum photo count met

---

## Notes

- Use glass cleaner and lint-free cloths or squeegee
- Clean both sides of glass when accessible
- Check for streaks by viewing from different angles
- Report damaged glass or hardware to admin via incident report
