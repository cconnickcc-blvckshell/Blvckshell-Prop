# Elevator Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_04  
**Area:** Elevator Cars  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **ELV-001** | Vacuum or sweep floor (no visible debris, dust, or pet hair) | Yes | Floor after cleaning | Visible debris, dust, or pet hair |
| **ELV-002** | Mop floor (if hard surface; streak-free, no residue) | Yes | Floor after mopping | Streaks or residue visible |
| **ELV-003** | Clean all walls (wipe down, no smudges or fingerprints) | Yes | Walls | Smudges or fingerprints visible |
| **ELV-004** | Clean control panel and buttons (sanitized, no smudges) | Yes | Control panel | Smudges or fingerprints |
| **ELV-005** | Clean handrails (sanitized, no smudges or sticky residue) | Yes | Handrails | Smudges or sticky residue |
| **ELV-006** | Clean ceiling and light fixtures (no visible dust or cobwebs) | Yes | Ceiling/fixtures | Dust or cobwebs visible |
| **ELV-007** | Clean mirror/reflective surfaces (streak-free, no smudges) | Yes | Mirror | Streaks or smudges |
| **ELV-008** | Ensure elevator is free of odors | Yes | N/A | Unpleasant odors present |
| **ELV-009** | Report any maintenance issues (burned bulbs, door issues, button malfunctions) | No | N/A | N/A (note if issues found) |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** ELV-001, ELV-002, ELV-004, ELV-005, ELV-007
- **2x/week:** ELV-003, ELV-006
- **Weekly:** ELV-008 (odor check)
- **As needed:** ELV-009 (maintenance reporting)

---

## Photo Requirements

**Minimum photos per visit:**
- 1 photo: Floor after cleaning (ELV-001 or ELV-002)
- 1 photo: Control panel (ELV-004)
- 1 photo: Walls (ELV-003)
- 1 photo: Mirror (ELV-007)

**Total minimum:** 4 photos per elevator visit

---

## Definition of Done

Elevator is considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- No visible debris, streaks, or smudges
- Control panel and buttons sanitized
- No unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met

---

## Notes

- **Safety:** Do not operate elevator during cleaning; use "service" or "hold" mode if available
- Control panel and buttons are high-touch surfaces; sanitize thoroughly
- Report elevator malfunctions immediately via incident report (do not attempt repairs)
- Be mindful of residents waiting; minimize disruption
