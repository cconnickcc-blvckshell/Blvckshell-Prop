# Hallway Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_02  
**Area:** Hallways / Corridors  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **HLY-001** | Sweep and mop floor (no visible debris, streaks, or residue) | Yes | Floor after cleaning | Visible debris, streaks, or residue |
| **HLY-002** | Vacuum carpets/rugs (no visible stains, debris, or pet hair) | Yes | Carpet areas | Stains, debris, or pet hair visible |
| **HLY-003** | Dust baseboards and door frames (no visible dust) | Yes | Baseboards | Dust visible |
| **HLY-004** | Wipe down light switches and door handles (sanitized, no smudges) | Yes | Switches/handles | Smudges or visible dirt |
| **HLY-005** | Clean wall-mounted fixtures (sconces, mirrors, art) | Yes | Main fixtures | Dust or smudges visible |
| **HLY-006** | Empty and clean waste/recycling bins (if present) | Yes | Bins after emptying | Bins not emptied or visibly dirty |
| **HLY-007** | Check for and remove cobwebs (corners, ceiling) | Yes | Corners/ceiling | Cobwebs visible |
| **HLY-008** | Ensure hallway is free of odors | Yes | N/A | Unpleasant odors present |
| **HLY-009** | Report any maintenance issues (burned bulbs, damaged walls) | No | N/A | N/A (note if issues found) |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** HLY-001, HLY-002, HLY-004, HLY-006
- **2x/week:** HLY-003, HLY-005, HLY-007
- **Weekly:** HLY-008 (odor check)
- **As needed:** HLY-009 (maintenance reporting)

---

## Photo Requirements

**Minimum photos per visit:**
- 1 photo: Floor after cleaning (HLY-001 or HLY-002)
- 1 photo: Baseboards (HLY-003)
- 1 photo: Waste bins if present (HLY-006)
- 1 photo: Overall hallway view

**Total minimum:** 4 photos per hallway visit

---

## Definition of Done

Hallway is considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- No visible debris, streaks, or smudges
- Bins emptied and clean (if present)
- No cobwebs or unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met

---

## Notes

- Use appropriate cleaning chemicals for floor type
- Be mindful of unit doors (do not disturb residents)
- Report maintenance issues (burned bulbs, damaged walls, water leaks) via incident report
