# Stairwell Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_03  
**Area:** Stairwells / Stairs  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **STW-001** | Sweep and mop all steps (no visible debris, streaks, or residue) | Yes | Steps after cleaning | Visible debris, streaks, or residue |
| **STW-002** | Clean handrails (sanitized, no smudges or sticky residue) | Yes | Handrails | Smudges or sticky residue |
| **STW-003** | Dust baseboards and risers (no visible dust) | Yes | Baseboards/risers | Dust visible |
| **STW-004** | Wipe down light switches (sanitized, no smudges) | Yes | Switches | Smudges or visible dirt |
| **STW-005** | Clean wall-mounted fixtures (sconces, mirrors) | Yes | Main fixtures | Dust or smudges visible |
| **STW-006** | Check for and remove cobwebs (corners, ceiling) | Yes | Corners/ceiling | Cobwebs visible |
| **STW-007** | Empty and clean waste bins (if present) | Yes | Bins after emptying | Bins not emptied or visibly dirty |
| **STW-008** | Ensure stairwell is free of odors | Yes | N/A | Unpleasant odors present |
| **STW-009** | Report any safety hazards (loose handrails, damaged steps, burned bulbs) | No | N/A | N/A (note if hazards found) |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** STW-001, STW-002, STW-004
- **2x/week:** STW-003, STW-005, STW-006
- **Weekly:** STW-007 (if bins present), STW-008 (odor check)
- **As needed:** STW-009 (safety hazard reporting)

---

## Photo Requirements

**Minimum photos per visit:**
- 1 photo: Steps after cleaning (STW-001)
- 1 photo: Handrails (STW-002)
- 1 photo: Baseboards/risers (STW-003)
- 1 photo: Overall stairwell view

**Total minimum:** 4 photos per stairwell visit

---

## Definition of Done

Stairwell is considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- No visible debris, streaks, or smudges on steps
- Handrails clean and sanitized
- No cobwebs or unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met

---

## Notes

- **Safety first:** Report loose handrails, damaged steps, or burned bulbs immediately via incident report
- Use appropriate cleaning chemicals for floor type (tile, concrete, carpet)
- Be mindful of residents using stairs during cleaning
- Handrails are high-touch surfaces; sanitize thoroughly
