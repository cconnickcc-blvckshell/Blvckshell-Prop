# Lobby Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_01  
**Area:** Lobby / Main Entrance  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **LOB-001** | Sweep and mop floor (no visible debris, streaks, or residue) | Yes | Floor after cleaning | Visible debris, streaks, or residue |
| **LOB-002** | Clean glass doors and windows (streak-free, no fingerprints) | Yes | Glass doors | Streaks or fingerprints visible |
| **LOB-003** | Wipe down reception desk/counter (clean, no dust or smudges) | Yes | Desk surface | Dust or smudges visible |
| **LOB-004** | Dust all surfaces (ledges, sills, decorative items) | Yes | Main surfaces | Dust visible on surfaces |
| **LOB-005** | Empty and clean waste/recycling bins | Yes | Bins after emptying | Bins not emptied or visibly dirty |
| **LOB-006** | Vacuum or spot-clean carpets/rugs (no visible stains or debris) | Yes | Carpet areas | Stains or debris visible |
| **LOB-007** | Clean light switches and door handles (sanitized, no smudges) | Yes | Switches/handles | Smudges or visible dirt |
| **LOB-008** | Check and replace light bulbs if burned out | No | N/A | N/A (note if bulbs need replacement) |
| **LOB-009** | Wipe down elevator call buttons and panels | Yes | Elevator panel | Smudges or fingerprints |
| **LOB-010** | Ensure lobby is free of odors | Yes | N/A | Unpleasant odors present |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** LOB-001, LOB-002, LOB-003, LOB-005, LOB-007, LOB-009
- **2x/week:** LOB-004, LOB-006
- **Weekly:** LOB-010 (odor check)
- **As needed:** LOB-008 (bulb replacement)

---

## Photo Requirements

**Minimum photos per visit:**
- 1 photo: Floor after cleaning (LOB-001)
- 1 photo: Glass doors (LOB-002)
- 1 photo: Reception desk (LOB-003)
- 1 photo: Waste bins (LOB-005)

**Total minimum:** 4 photos per lobby visit

---

## Definition of Done

Lobby is considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- No visible debris, streaks, or smudges
- Bins emptied and clean
- No unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met

---

## Notes

- Use appropriate cleaning chemicals for floor type (tile, hardwood, carpet)
- Be mindful of residents/visitors during cleaning
- Report any maintenance issues (burned bulbs, broken fixtures) to admin
