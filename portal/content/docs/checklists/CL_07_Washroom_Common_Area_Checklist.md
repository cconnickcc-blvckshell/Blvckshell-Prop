# Washroom Common Area Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_07  
**Area:** Common Area Washrooms / Restrooms  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **WCR-001** | Sweep and mop floor (no visible debris, streaks, or residue) | Yes | Floor after cleaning | Visible debris, streaks, or residue |
| **WCR-002** | Clean and sanitize toilets (inside and out; no visible stains or residue) | Yes | Toilets after cleaning | Stains or residue visible |
| **WCR-003** | Clean and sanitize sinks and countertops (no visible stains, water spots, or residue) | Yes | Sinks/countertops | Stains, water spots, or residue visible |
| **WCR-004** | Clean mirrors (streak-free, no water spots or smudges) | Yes | Mirrors | Streaks, water spots, or smudges |
| **WCR-005** | Clean and sanitize faucets and handles (no water spots or residue) | Yes | Faucets/handles | Water spots or residue |
| **WCR-006** | Restock paper towels, toilet paper, soap (if supplies provided by company) | Yes | Dispensers | Empty dispensers (if company-provided supplies) |
| **WCR-007** | Empty and clean waste bins (sanitized, no overflow) | Yes | Bins after emptying | Bins not emptied or visibly dirty |
| **WCR-008** | Clean and sanitize door handles and light switches | Yes | Handles/switches | Smudges or visible dirt |
| **WCR-009** | Clean walls (wipe down, no splatters or residue) | Yes | Walls | Splatters or residue visible |
| **WCR-010** | Ensure washroom is free of odors (use deodorizer if needed) | Yes | N/A | Unpleasant odors present |
| **WCR-011** | Report any maintenance issues (leaks, broken fixtures, burned bulbs) | No | N/A | N/A (note if issues found) |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Daily:** WCR-001, WCR-002, WCR-003, WCR-004, WCR-005, WCR-007, WCR-008, WCR-010
- **2x/week:** WCR-009
- **As needed:** WCR-006 (restocking), WCR-011 (maintenance reporting)

---

## Photo Requirements

**Minimum photos per visit:**
- 1 photo: Floor after cleaning (WCR-001) — **REQUIRED**
- 1 photo: Toilets after cleaning (WCR-002) — **REQUIRED**
- 1 photo: Sinks/countertops (WCR-003)
- 1 photo: Mirrors (WCR-004)

**Total minimum:** 4 photos per washroom visit (floor and toilets are mandatory)

---

## Definition of Done

Washroom is considered clean when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- Floor free of debris, streaks, and residue
- Toilets and sinks cleaned and sanitized
- Mirrors streak-free
- Bins emptied and sanitized
- No unpleasant odors
- Minimum photo count met (especially floor and toilets)

---

## Notes

- **High priority:** Washrooms are high-traffic, high-sanitation areas; thorough cleaning is critical
- Use appropriate cleaning chemicals and sanitizers
- Wear appropriate PPE (gloves) when cleaning washrooms
- Report maintenance issues (leaks, broken fixtures) immediately via incident report
- Restocking: Only if supplies are provided by company (per site agreement)
