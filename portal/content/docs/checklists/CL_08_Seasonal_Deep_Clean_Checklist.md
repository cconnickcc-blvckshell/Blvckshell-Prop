# Seasonal Deep Clean Checklist

**Checklist ID:** CL_08  
**Area:** All Common Areas (Deep Clean)  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Date:** February 16, 2026

---

## Purpose

This checklist covers deep cleaning tasks performed seasonally (quarterly or as scheduled) in addition to regular maintenance cleaning. Deep cleans address areas not covered in daily/weekly routines.

---

## Checklist Items

| Item ID | Task | Required | Photo Point | Fail Condition |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|----------------|
| **DCL-001** | Deep clean floors (scrub, strip, and refinish if applicable; or deep vacuum/shampoo carpets) | Yes | Floors after deep clean | Floors not properly deep cleaned |
| **DCL-002** | Clean baseboards thoroughly (remove all dust, scuff marks, residue) | Yes | Baseboards | Dust, scuff marks, or residue visible |
| **DCL-003** | Clean walls (spot clean marks, scuffs, splatters; full wash if needed) | Yes | Walls | Marks, scuffs, or splatters visible |
| **DCL-004** | Clean ceiling fixtures and vents (remove dust, cobwebs, debris) | Yes | Ceiling/fixtures | Dust, cobwebs, or debris visible |
| **DCL-005** | Deep clean windows and window sills (inside and out if accessible) | Yes | Windows/sills | Streaks, dust, or residue visible |
| **DCL-006** | Clean and polish all metal fixtures (door handles, railings, elevator panels) | Yes | Metal fixtures | Tarnish, smudges, or residue visible |
| **DCL-007** | Deep clean garbage rooms (scrub floors, walls, bins; remove all residue and odors) | Yes | Garbage room | Residue or odors present |
| **DCL-008** | Deep clean washrooms (scrub grout, clean behind fixtures, remove all buildup) | Yes | Washroom deep clean | Buildup or residue visible |
| **DCL-009** | Clean and organize storage areas (if accessible) | No | Storage areas | N/A (note if areas cleaned) |
| **DCL-010** | Report any maintenance issues discovered during deep clean | No | N/A | N/A (note if issues found) |

---

## Frequency Guidance

- **Quarterly:** DCL-001, DCL-002, DCL-003, DCL-004, DCL-005, DCL-006
- **Semi-annually:** DCL-007, DCL-008
- **As scheduled:** DCL-009, DCL-010

## Definition of Done

Deep clean is considered complete when:
- All required checklist items marked PASS
- Floors properly deep cleaned (scrubbed/stripped or deep vacuumed/shampooed)
- Baseboards, walls, and ceilings free of dust, marks, and residue
- Windows clean inside and out (if accessible)
- Metal fixtures polished and free of tarnish
- Garbage rooms and washrooms deep cleaned
- Minimum photo count met

---

## Notes

- **Scheduling:** Deep cleans are scheduled separately from regular maintenance; coordinate with admin
- **Time allocation:** Deep cleans require more time than regular cleaning; plan accordingly
- **Supplies:** May require additional supplies (stripper, polish, deep cleaning chemicals); coordinate with admin
- **Access:** Some areas (storage, exterior windows) may require special access; coordinate with admin
- **Reporting:** Document any maintenance issues discovered during deep clean via incident report

---

## Integration with Regular Checklists

Deep clean checklist items complement regular checklists (CL_01 through CL_07). Regular checklists cover daily/weekly maintenance; this checklist covers quarterly/semi-annual deep cleaning tasks.

**Example:** Regular lobby cleaning (CL_01) covers daily mopping; deep clean (DCL-001) covers quarterly floor stripping/refinishing.
